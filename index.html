My story

Born and brought up in a family of creative heads, I have always been involved in art and science. My love for technology and creativity only grew with time.

I've always been a keen and creative individual but one thing I was unsure about was how to use technology to bring my artistic visions to reality. During my under graduation, I interned as a Data Analyst and subsequently worked as a Software Engineer and Social Media Analyst at Tech Mahindra where I learned about the power of data in today's world. During my three years in the IT industry, I discovered a great passion for working with and experimenting with data. I then focused on the various options available to me in order to determine which path would be best for me, eventually deciding on Data Science, Analytics, and Visualization.
So, for Summer 2020, I volunteered as an illustrator for the 'North Texas Daily,' my university's newspaper. Throughout the process, I learned a lot of new things, such as how to make the transition from conventional to digital art, how to grow as an artist, and, most significantly, how to translate an article into an illustration, which is precisely what is needed to stand out in this Data-driven world . I feel it encouraged me in broadening my possibilities and changing my viewpoint on things. And in the end, it all makes sense. The world of Data Science and Visualization brings out the best in me in many ways.
