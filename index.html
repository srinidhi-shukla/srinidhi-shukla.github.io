My story

Born and brought up in a family of creative heads, I have always been involved in art and science. My love for technology and creativity grew with time.

One thing I was unsure about was how to use technology to bring my artistic imagination to life. I worked as a Software Engineer at Tech Mahindra for almost two years after finishing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I focused on the various options available to me in order to determine which direction would be better for me. In many ways, the world of Data Science and Visualization brings out the best of me. So, for Summer 2020, I volunteered as an illustrator for my university's newspaper, the 'North Texas Daily.' Throughout the process, I learned a lot of new things, like how the transition from traditional art to digital art is done, how to evolve as an artist, and most importantly, how to translate an article into an illustration, which is exactly what is required when visualizing data. I believe it helped me in widening my possibilities and changing my perspective on things. And it all makes sense in the end.
